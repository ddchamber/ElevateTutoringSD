About Us

Elevate was founded by educators who believe in more than just academic success-we believe in helping students grow into confident, curious, and capable learners. With decades of experience, we know how to reach learners at every stage and help them rise to their full potential.

Meet Suzi

With over 20 years of experience in public education teaching grades K–8, Suzi has dedicated her career to helping children grow in confidence and thrive academically. In addition to her classroom expertise, she has spent the last three years serving as a homeschool mentor teacher, coming alongside parents to support and guide. Suzi’s passion for teaching and tutoring shines through in her personalized approach, where she not only helps students master academic skills but also fosters self-esteem and a love for learning. Her heart for children and belief in their potential make her a trusted partner in education for both students and families.

Meet Libby

Libby is a newly credentialed educator with a passion that shines brightly in every classroom and tutoring session. With three years of hands-on experience, she has already built a reputation for her ability to truly connect with children, making learning both meaningful and joyful. Libby’s warm and encouraging approach not only helps students grow academically, but also supports their social and emotional development.
